Activities

We are a very active troop, going on an outing every month and to summer camp at Long Lake in July.  We have been meeting at Lost Arrow Campsite for week 3 as long as any active members can remember.  In the summer of 2004, our high adventure group became certified as Open Water Scuba Divers, and now as new Venture Crew 53 members, have performed more dives and attending the Sea Base Scuba Adventure in 2005, 2007, and will be going this summer of  2009. 

In 2004,  we took a white water rafting expedition to West Virginia.  In 2006,  we took a 50 mile canoe trek down the Rifle River in Michigan.  The summer of 2008, we canoed the Boundary Waters of northern Minnesota.

    Order of the Arrow Spring Conference
    Camp Long Lake
    June 11-13, 2004

    Kenton Chodara and Sean Ryan
    attend the Conference
    Sean leads new candidates 
    to the start of the Ordeal

    The OA Spring conference was held at Camp Long Lake the weekend of June 11-13.  Kenton Chodara performed the Ordeal and was inducted into the Order along with 34 others.  Troop committee member and Council Advancement Chairman Andy Stockhausen was called out for Vigil Honor, and kept his vigil.  He was awarded Vigil Honor on Saturday, June 12.  Sean Ryan and Mr. Ryan also participated in the conference.


    New OA Vigil Honor Recipients Jean Guzzetta from Troop 71 in Brookfield, Jon Rzeznik from Troop 63 in Waukesha, Troop 53 Committee Member Andy Stockhausen, and Tony Giles from Troop 246 in Brookfield.
